Unit 6 句型汇总

0 / 20
Subject 主语 (S)
Verb 谓语 (V)
Object 宾语 (O)
Complement 补语 (C)
Sentence 01
SVO Decl+
These orebodies will certainly require shafts and levels and probably declines for access for machinery between levels.
大型金属矿床需要竖井和水平巷道，同时在中段之间还可能开掘斜坡道方便机械设备上下转移。
主语 These orebodies + 谓语 require + 宾语 shafts and levels...。will certainly 为助动词+副词修饰谓语，表示肯定推测。宾语为并列名词短语，declines 后接 for 短语作定语。
Sentence 02
SVO Decl+
If one has to opt for a modern development as typical, then Cobar Mines is probably very representative.
如果要选择一个典型的现代矿山开拓实例，卡巴矿就是一个很好的代表。
If 引导条件状语从句，主句为 SVC 结构（主系表）。opt for 意为"选择"，representative 为形容词作表语。probably very 为副词+程度副词修饰表语。
Sentence 03
SVO Complex Decl+
Its benefits have been such to the company that before the metal price crisis of the mid-1970s Cobar had deepened both shafts and added a third major haulage level.
矿山收益如此之大，以至于在20世纪70年代中期金属价格低迷之前，卡巴矿就已经延伸了竖井，并增设了第三个主运输中段。
such...that... 为结果状语从句结构。主句为 SVC 结构，such 为表语。从句中 Cobar 为主语，had deepened 和 added 为并列谓语（过去完成时）。
Sentence 04
SVP Decl+
The interval between levels depends on geology and size of orebody, strength of backs and walls, mining methods, life of ore passes, capital available, access for men, current technology, and surveying convenience.
两中段之间的高度取决于矿床地质条件和矿床大小、矿床顶底板和侧面岩石强度、采矿方法、矿石溜井使用期限、资金量、人员通道、当前的技术水平和便于勘探等因素。
主语 The interval between levels + 谓语 depends on（动词短语）+ 宾语（八个并列名词短语）。depends on 为固定搭配，意为"取决于"。宾语为复杂的并列结构。
Sentence 05
SVO Passive Decl+
A badly faulted and disturbed orebody will need many exploration levels and diamond drill holes.
断层带错动的矿体需要在多个水平进行勘探和钻探来探明矿体。
主语 faulted and disturbed orebody（形容词+并列形容词+名词）+ 谓语 will need + 宾语 many exploration levels...。faulted 和 disturbed 为过去分词作前置定语修饰 orebody。
Sentence 06
SVO Passive Decl+
A large orebody which really consists of many scattered lenses of variable grade will have to be developed with a close level interval and mined selectively.
由多个分散的透镜状矿体组成的大型矿体的开拓水平之间的高度要降低，而且要进行选择性开采。
主语 A large orebody + which 定语从句修饰 + 谓语 will have to be developed and mined（被动语态并列谓语）。with a close level interval 为方式状语，selectively 为副词修饰 mined。
Sentence 07
SVO Decl+
Size of orebody will control levels on a simple multiple basis.
根据开拓水平高度和矿体延伸尺寸就可以确定矿床开拓水平数量。
主语 Size of orebody + 谓语 will control + 宾语 levels。on a simple multiple basis 为介词短语作方式状语，说明控制的方式。
Sentence 08
SVO Complex Decl+
If the orebody is 400m deep, even though a 180m level interval is feasible, it may be conveniently split into three.
如果矿体垂直延伸400m，尽管矿床开拓可以采用阶段高度180m，那么设计两个中段进行开拓就不能满足矿床开拓的需要，因此，需要设计成三个中段进行开拓。
If 引导条件状语从句，even though 引导让步状语从句。主句为 SVO 结构，it 指代 orebody，may be split 为被动语态谓语，into three 为介词短语作结果状语。
Sentence 09
SVO Decl+
A strong ore and strong country rock enable large open stopes to be worked.
矿石与围岩强度大，则采场空间就大。
主语 strong ore and strong country rock（并列名词短语）+ 谓语 enable + 宾语 large open stopes + to be worked（不定式作宾语补足语）。enable sb./sth. to do 为固定搭配。
Sentence 10
SVO Decl+
Stopes should be worked as high as possible because that reduce the cost of drawpoints and chutes to a minimum for the mine.
采场高度也应该尽量大一些，因为采场的高度高，可以减少掘进出矿口和溜槽数量，从而降低成本。
主句为被动语态 SVO 结构，should be worked。as high as possible 为方式状语。because 引导原因状语从句，从句中 that reduce... 为 SVO 结构，reduce... to a minimum 为固定搭配。
Sentence 11
SVO Decl+
Cut and fill methods enable large intervals to be obtained between levels.
采用充填法采矿，可以增加中段高度。
主语 Cut and fill methods + 谓语 enable + 宾语 large intervals + to be obtained（不定式被动式作宾语补足语）。between levels 为介词短语作定语修饰 intervals。
Sentence 12
SVO Passive Decl+
Ring-drilled holes must overlap about 1.5 to 2m to obtain good fragmentation.
环形炮孔之间需要相互重叠约1.5~2m才能获得良好的破碎效果。
主语 Ring-drilled holes（过去分词作定语）+ 谓语 must overlap + 宾语 about 1.5 to 2m。to obtain good fragmentation 为不定式作目的状语。
Sentence 13
SVO Passive Decl+
Block caving tends to increase the spacing between levels.
阶段崩落法的发展趋势是增加中段高度。
主语 Block caving + 谓语 tends to increase（动词短语）+ 宾语 the spacing between levels。tends to 为固定搭配，意为"倾向于"。between levels 为介词短语作定语。
Sentence 14
SVO Passive Decl+
A level interval of 150 to 180m is easily attainable.
中段高度可以达到150~180m。
主语 A level interval of 150 to 180m + 系动词 is + 表语 attainable（形容词）。easily 为副词修饰 attainable。此句为 SVC 结构，表示状态的被动形式。
Sentence 15
SVO Passive Decl+
In most stopping methods ore is drawn off through an ore pass at some stage.
在大多数采矿方法中，矿石在某个阶段需要通过矿石溜井进行放矿。
In most stopping methods 为介词短语作状语。主语 ore + 谓语 is drawn off（被动语态动词短语）+ through an ore pass 为方式状语 + at some stage 为时间状语。
Sentence 16
SVO Decl+
Once the life of an ore pass has been determined in tonnes, the cost can be determined.
当溜井的使用寿命（以矿石吨数计算）确定后，就可以确定溜井间距与长度的成本。
Once 引导时间状语从句，从句为被动语态。主句主语 the cost + 谓语 can be determined（被动语态）。此句为典型的科技英语被动句型。
Sentence 17
SVO Decl+
The fewer the ore passes in relation to tramming runs, the lower the cost.
溜井数量越少，运输距离越短，成本就越低。
此句为 "the + 比较级..., the + 比较级..." 结构，表示"越...就越..."。前半部分为比较状语，后半部分为主句。in relation to tramming runs 为介词短语修饰 ore passes。
Sentence 18
SVO Modal Decl+
If capital is difficult to raise, small equipment will be used.
如果资金筹措困难，则只能使用小型设备。
If 引导条件状语从句。主句主语 small equipment + 谓语 will be used（被动语态）。difficult to raise 为形容词+不定式结构作表语。
Sentence 19
SVO Passive Decl+
High capital expenditure has to be justified by large outputs.
高资本投入需要大规模生产来证明其合理性。
主语 High capital expenditure + 谓语 has to be justified（情态动词+被动语态）+ by large outputs 为方式状语（动作执行者）。justify 意为"证明...是合理的"。
Sentence 20
SVO Passive Decl+
Raise-bored holes of 300m or more between levels are feasible.
300m或更长的天井钻进已成为可行。
主语 Raise-bored holes + of 300m or more between levels（介词短语作定语）+ 系动词 are + 表语 feasible（形容词）。此句为 SVC 结构，表示状态的可行性。
