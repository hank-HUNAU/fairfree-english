Level Interval Design Discussion
中段间距设计讨论
Dr.Z
Good morning, Mr. Li. Today we need to discuss the level interval中段间距 design for our new project. What factors should we consider?
早上好，李先生。今天我们需要讨论新项目的中段间距设计。我们应该考虑哪些因素？
Based on the textbook, the interval间距 between levels depends on eight factors. The most important ones are geology地质条件 and size of orebody, strength of backs and walls顶底板和侧墙, and mining methods.
根据教材，中段间距取决于八个因素。最重要的是矿床地质条件和大小、顶底板和侧墙强度以及采矿方法。

Dr.Z
Excellent. Our orebody is badly faulted断层错动的 and has variable grade品位. What level interval would you recommend?

Drilling Technology and Sub-level Height
钻孔技术与分段高度讨论
Dr.Z
Mr. Li, what are the limitations of current ring drilling环形钻孔 technology for our sublevel open stopping分段空场法?
李先生，当前环形钻孔技术对我们的分段空场法有什么限制？
Small blastholes of about 50mm tend to deviate偏斜 at depths of 15m. At 20m depth, deviation may reach 0.3m. This can cause poor fragmentation破碎效果 or complete failure to break a ring.
约50mm的小直径炮孔在15m深度处开始偏斜。在20m深度处，偏斜可能达到0.3m。这可能导致破碎效果差或完全无法破碎一个环形炮孔。
Mr.L
Dr.Z
What about down-the-hole hammer drills潜孔锤钻 with larger diameters? Can they help extend the drilling range?
直径更大的潜孔锤钻怎么样？它们能帮助延长钻孔范围吗？
Yes, drills with 100 to 150mm bit diameters can drill 20 to 30m without noticeable deviation. The impact at the bottom reduces rod whip钻杆摆动. Hole lengths of 50 to 60m are possible.
是的，直径100到150mm的钻头可以钻进20到30m而无明显偏差。孔底的冲击作用减少了钻杆摆动。孔深可达50到60m。

Ore Pass System Design
矿石溜井系统设计讨论
Dr.Z
Let's discuss the ore pass矿石溜井 system. What factors affect the life of ore passes?
让我们讨论矿石溜井系统。哪些因素影响溜井的使用寿命？
Ore passes can be through ore, through rock, or a lined pass内衬溜井 through fill. All have finite life. When enlarged, they become unsafe with subsequent cave-ins冒落 or slabbing片帮 causing heavy dilution贫化.
溜井可以在矿体内、岩体内，或充填体内的内衬溜井。所有溜井都有一定的使用寿命。当断面扩大后变得不安全，随后发生冒落或片帮导致严重贫化。
Mr.L
Dr.Z
How should we optimize the ore pass layout to reduce tramming runs电机车运输 costs?
我们应该如何优化溜井布置以降低电机车运输成本？
Near-vertical ore passes in rock can concentrate output from several stopes into a central ore bin矿仓 close to the shaft. Ore can run through a primary crusher初级破碎机 and onto a conveyor for automated hoisting.
岩体内近垂直的溜井可以将多个采场的矿石集中到靠近竖井的中央矿仓。矿石可以通过初级破碎机，然后输送到皮带输送机实现自动化提升。

Raise Boring Technology Advancement
天井钻进技术进步讨论
Dr.Z
How has raise boring天井钻进 technology changed level interval design in modern mines?
天井钻进技术如何改变了现代矿山的中段间距设计？
Older mines using hand-raising人工掘进天井 had intervals of only 15 to 20m. With Alimak阿利马克爬罐 climbers, intervals increased to 30m then 50m. Now raise-bored holes天井钻孔 of 300m or more are feasible.
采用人工掘进天井的老矿山间距只有15到20m。使用阿利马克爬罐后，间距增加到30m再到50m。现在300m或更长的天井钻孔已成为可行。
Mr.L
Dr.Z
What is the most economic development design today? Can you give an example?
目前最经济的开拓设计是什么？你能举个例子吗？
Cobar Mines is a good example. It has major levels at 180m intervals with a decline斜坡道 access ramp. Fewer levels mean more investment per level and better ore pass矿石溜井 development.
卡巴矿是一个很好的例子。它的主要中段间距为180m，配有斜坡道通道。中段越少，每个中段的投入越多，矿石溜井建设也更好。
