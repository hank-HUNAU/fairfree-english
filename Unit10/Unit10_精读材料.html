Introduction 引言
Paragraph 1
Block caving is a mass mining method大规模采矿方法 in which the ore body is undercut and allowed to cave崩落 under its own weight.
阶段崩落法是一种大规模采矿方法，矿体被切割后在自重作用下崩落。
Paragraph 2
The method is applicable to large, massive orebodies with weak ore and rock. The ore must be fragmented破碎 sufficiently to flow through drawpoints出矿口.
该方法适用于大型、整体矿体，矿石和岩石强度较低。矿石必须破碎到足够程度才能通过出矿口流出。
Method 方法
Paragraph 3
An undercut切割层 is developed at the base of the orebody. The undercut removes support and initiates the caving process崩落过程.
在矿体底部开掘切割层。切割层移除支撑并启动崩落过程。
Paragraph 4
Drawpoints are located below the undercut level. Broken ore is drawn off through these drawpoints出矿口 at a controlled rate.
出矿口位于切割层下方。破碎矿石以控制的速度通过这些出矿口放出。
Advantages 优点
Paragraph 5
Block caving is a low-cost method with high production rates. It requires minimal development work and has low labor requirements劳动力需求.
阶段崩落法是一种低成本、高产量的方法。它需要最少的开拓工程，劳动力需求低。
Paragraph 6
The method is suitable for large orebodies with weak ore and rock. However, it requires careful draw control放矿控制 to minimize dilution贫化.
该方法适用于矿石和岩石强度较低的大型矿体。然而，它需要仔细的放矿控制以最小化贫化。
