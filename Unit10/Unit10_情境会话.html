Shrinkage Stopping Principle
留矿法原理讨论
Dr.Z
What is the basic support mechanism in shrinkage stopping?
留矿法的基本支护机制是什么？
The support is broken ore破碎矿石 left in the stope. When the stope is filled with broken ore破碎矿石, the fractured rock is restrained约束 from falling.
支护是留在采场内的破碎矿石。当采场充满破碎矿石时，破裂岩石被约束不会掉落。
Mr.L
Dr.Z
What is the swell factor碎胀系数 and why is it important?
什么是碎胀系数，为什么它很重要？
The swell factor碎胀系数 is about 1.3 to 1.5. Without this swell, there would be no output during mining.
碎胀系数约1.3到1.5。没有碎胀，采矿期间就无法出矿。
Mr.L
Key Vocabulary
broken ore 破碎矿石
swell factor 碎胀系数
restrained 约束
fragmentation 破碎
2
Vertical Stopping Method
垂直采矿方法讨论
Dr.Z
What makes shrinkage stopping a vertical stopping垂直采矿 method?
什么使留矿法成为垂直采矿方法？
The stope is carried on in a vertical or near-vertical plane at an angle greater than the angle of repose安息角 of the broken ore.
采场在垂直或近似垂直面上进行，角度大于破碎矿石的安息角。
Mr.L
Dr.Z
How does shrinkage stopping provide working space for miners?
留矿法如何为矿工提供作业空间？
30% to 40% of broken ore is drawn off during mining to provide working space. The remaining ore serves as temporary support and working platform作业平台.
采矿期间放出30%到40%的破碎矿石提供作业空间。剩余矿石作为临时支护和作业平台。
Mr.L
Key Vocabulary
vertical stopping 垂直采矿
angle of repose 安息角
working platform 作业平台
overhand method 上向式采矿
3
Crown Pillar Support
顶柱支护讨论
Dr.Z
How should we support the crown pillar顶柱 in shrinkage stopping?
留矿法中如何支护顶柱？
The backs under the crown pillar顶柱 may be rock-bolted岩石锚杆支护 to increase stability and avoid premature collapse过早冒落 when drawing off ores.
顶柱下方可以采用岩石锚杆支护，增加稳定性，避免出矿时过早冒落。
Mr.L
Dr.Z
What measures can be taken to suppress dust during ore drawing?
出矿期间可以采取什么措施抑制粉尘？
Water sprays can be installed on the backs before the stope is drawn off to help suppress dust at draw points.
出矿前可以在顶板安装喷水设备，抑制出矿口的粉尘。
Mr.L
Key Vocabulary
crown pillar 顶柱
rock-bolted 岩石锚杆支护
premature collapse 过早冒落
dust suppression 粉尘抑制
