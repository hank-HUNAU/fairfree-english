Paragraph 1
It may appear rather obvious to state that all mine accesses have two ends, a top and a bottom. However, the point is made to remind readers that both ends have to be considered. For an inclined access in a metalliferous mine there is really no problem because an offsetoffset distance between top and bottom is easily arranged.
很明显所有矿山井筒都有两个端口：一个顶部端口，一个底部端口。两个端口都得考虑周全。对于金属矿山的斜井而言，顶底部之间的偏移距离可以很容易进行调整，因此斜井不存在实际问题。
Paragraph 2
Briefly the requirements for surface location are: (1) Sufficient flat land to establish the mine surface complex; (2) As close as possible to railway, roads and power lines; (3) Adequate safe disposal areas close by for mullockmullock and tailingtailing.
简言之，工业广场在地表位置的选择应满足如下要求：（1）地表具有足够的平整土地，可以布置矿山工业设施；（2）工业广场尽量布置在靠近铁路、公路或动力线的地方；（3）工业广场附近有足够的容纳废石和尾砂的安全场地。
Paragraph 3
Plant needing protective pillarprotective pillars should be sited above the worst geological area (faultfaults, thin coal, dykesdykes, etc). Put the pillar over the shallowest part of the seam or orebody because then the protective pillarprotective pillar is smallest and the shaft or decline is also shorter.
需要保安矿柱保护的设施应布置在最差的地质区域上方（断层、薄煤层、岩脉等）。将矿柱布置在矿层或矿体最浅的部分上方，因为这样保安矿柱最小，竖井或斜井也最短。
Paragraph 4
Avoid placing any surface plant close to the mine. Costs may be such that if material is belt-hauledbelt-hauled or truck-hauled off the leaselease to a central process plant then the total running cost of a complex could be lower.
避免将任何地表设施布置在靠近矿山的地方。成本可能使得如果材料通过带式运输或卡车运输离开租地到中央处理厂，则整个综合设施的运行成本可能更低。
Paragraph 5
The concept of centre of gravitycentre of gravity has been frequently suggested that a shaft should be sited at the centre of gravitycentre of gravity of an orebody. The basic premise is that the cost of haulagehaulage of development rock and of mineral should be minimized.
重心概念经常有人建议竖井应布置在矿体的重心位置。基本前提是开拓岩石和矿物的运输成本应最小化。
