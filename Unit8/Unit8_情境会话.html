Room and Pillar Layout
房柱法布置讨论
Dr.Z
What is the basic layout principle of room and pillar mining?
房柱法的基本布置原则是什么？
Openings are driven orthogonally正交布置 and at regular intervals, forming rectangular or square pillars矿柱 for natural support.
采场进路采用正交布置，间距相等，形成长方形或正方形矿柱支撑地压。

Dr.Z
What are the advantages of driving several openings at one time?
同时掘进多条进路有什么优点？
It increases production and efficiency, and improves ventilation通风 and transportation.
提供多个作业工作面，增加生产能力和效率，同时改善通风和运输条件。

Conventional vs Continuous Mining
传统开采与连续开采
Dr.Z
What is the production cycle生产循环 in conventional room and pillar mining?
传统房柱法的生产循环是什么？
The production cycle生产循环 is: cut + drill + blast + load + haul. It requires at least 5 working places.
生产循环是：切割 + 钻孔 + 爆破 + 装载 + 运输。需要至少5个工作面。
Mr.L
Dr.Z
How is the cycle simplified in continuous mining连续开采?
连续开采的循环如何简化？
In continuous mining连续开采, the cycle is simplified to: mine + haul. A continuous miner连续采煤机 breaks and loads simultaneously.
连续开采的循环简化为：开采 + 运输。连续采煤机同时破碎和装载。

Pillar Design
矿柱设计讨论
Dr.Z
How should we determine the pillar width矿柱宽度 to avoid stress super-positioning应力集中?
如何确定矿柱宽度以避免应力集中？
A pillar width矿柱宽度 equal to at least three times the opening width is required, especially for long-life entries.
矿柱宽度至少要等于三倍矿房宽度，特别是对于永久巷道。
Mr.L
Dr.Z
What happens when an opening is created?
当采场形成时会发生什么？
The unit weight of overburden覆盖层 is transferred by an arching action拱形作用 to the sides of the opening or adjacent pillars.
上覆岩层的重力以拱形方式转移到采场周围的矿柱和侧墙。
