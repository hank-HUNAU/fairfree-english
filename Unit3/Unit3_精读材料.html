Paragraph 1
Types of underground development openings can be ranked in three categories by order of importance in the overall layout of the mine: (1) Primary: main openings (e.g., shaftshaft, slopeslope); (2) Secondary: level or zone openings (e.g., driftdrift, entryentry); (3) Tertiary: lateral or panel openings (e.g., rampramp, crosscutcrosscut).
地下开采的开拓工程根据其重要程度可分为三类：（1）主要开拓工程（如：竖井，斜井）；（2）水平中段巷道或采场巷道（如：平巷，进路）；（3）辅助巷道或盘区巷道（如：斜坡道，切割巷道）。
Paragraph 2
Generally, the openings are driven in this order; that is, from main development openings to secondary level or zone openings, to tertiary openings like lateral or panel openings. However, many variations exist with different mining methods.
通常地下工程是从主要开拓工程开始向水平中段巷道或采场巷道方向进行掘进，再向辅助巷道或盘区巷道进行掘进。但是，不同的采矿方法其掘进的先后次序也发生变化。
Paragraph 3
Mine development must proceed considering all aspects of the overall mine design. Because of the complexity and expense of underground mining, extreme care must be exercised in making decisions during development that may also affect subsequent production operations.
矿山开拓必须综合考虑矿山整体设计的各个方面。由于地下开采的复杂性和高成本，在开拓阶段做出决策时必须极为谨慎，因为这些决策可能会影响后续的生产作业。
Paragraph 4
Mining method selection is crucial because it largely governs the type and placement of the primary development openings. If disturbance of the surface due to subsidencesubsidence is anticipated, then all the access openings must be located outside the zone of fracture bounded by the angle of draw.
采矿方法选择之所以如此关键，是因为它很大程度上决定了主要开拓巷道的类型和位置。如果预计地表会因沉陷而受到扰动，那么所有通道都必须位于由边界角确定的破裂区之外。
Paragraph 5
In regard to types of openings, there are only three common choices: (1) shaftshafts (vertical and near-vertical); (2) declinedeclines (slopeslopes or rampramps); (3) aditadits or driftdrifts. Vertical shaftshafts have always been among the most common types of openings for deep mines.
关于巷道类型，常用的选择只有三种：（1）竖井（垂直和近垂直）；（2）斜坡道（斜井或坡道）；（3）平硐或平巷。竖井一直是深矿井最常见的巷道类型之一。
