Sentence-01
SVO Decl+
Types of underground development openings can be ranked in three categories.
地下开拓巷道类型可按三类排序。
主语 Types + 谓语 can be ranked + 宾语 in three categories（介词短语作状语）
Sentence-02
SVO Passive Decl+
The openings are driven in this order.
巷道按此顺序掘进。
被动语态：主语 openings + 被动谓语 are driven + 状语 in this order
Sentence-03
SVO Modal Decl+
Mine development must proceed considering all aspects of the overall mine design.
矿山开拓必须综合考虑矿山整体设计的各个方面。
情态动词 must + 谓语 proceed + 状语 considering...
Sentence-04
SVO Q-Spec
What factors influence the selection of mining method?
什么因素影响采矿方法的选择？
特殊疑问句：What + 谓语 influence + 宾语 the selection...
Sentence-05
SVP Decl+
Vertical shafts have always been among the most common types of openings.
竖井一直是最常见的巷道类型之一。
主系表结构：主语 shafts + 系动词 have been + 表语 among...
Sentence-06
SVO Passive Imp
Note that the shaft must be located outside the zone of fracture.
注意竖井必须位于破裂区之外。
祈使句：Note that + 从句（从句中为被动语态）
