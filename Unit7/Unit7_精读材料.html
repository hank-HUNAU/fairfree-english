Introduction 引言
Paragraph 1
This chapter will deal with orebodies that have significant vertical dimensions. It is essentially three-dimensional mining in which lateral development roadway横向开拓巷道s are driven at successive depths and connected vertically.
本章讲述垂直延伸矿体的开采。在矿床的三维开采空间中，需要在各个中段掘进水平开拓巷道与竖井相通。
Paragraph 2
Mining then proceeds upwards in overhand stopping上向式采场, or downwards in underhand stopping下向式采场. Steeply inclined thin orebodies or massive bodies are worked on these general methods.
采场中的采矿作业可以采用上向式开采，也可以采用下向式开采。急倾斜矿体或大型矿体可以采用这样的开采方法。
Paragraph 3
A change in some thin bodies from working along the strike走向 to working down dip倾斜 is made when the footwall dip倾斜s at less than approximately 45 degrees.
当某些薄矿体底板倾斜角度小于45度，则作业方式需要调整。
Classification 分类
Paragraph 4
Underground mining methods are categorized in three classes: unsupported空场法, supported充填法 and caving崩落法. The unsupported空场法 class consists of those methods which are essentially self-supporting.
地下开采方法分为三大类：空场法、充填法和崩落法。空场法主要包括几种采矿法，这几种空场采矿法基本靠自身支撑地压。
Paragraph 5
The superincumbent load上覆岩层载荷 is comprised of the weight of the overburden and any tectonic构造的 forces acting at depth.
采场上部荷载是由上覆岩层自重和构造应力组成。
Paragraph 6
The unsupported class accounts for over 80% of the U.S. mineral production from subsurface mines. The four main unsupported methods are: room and pillar房柱法, stope and pillar, shrinkage stopping留矿法, and sublevel stopping.
空场法占美国地下矿产产量的80%以上。四种主要空场法是：房柱法、全面法、留矿法和分段空场法。
