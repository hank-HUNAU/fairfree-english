Unit 9 精读材料
Introduction 引言
Paragraph 1
Sublevel stopping is an unsupported method空场法 applied to relatively steeply dipping, large orebodies. The method is characterized by the use of sublevels分段 within the stope.
分段空场法是一种用于开采急倾斜大型矿体的空场采矿法。该方法的特点是在采场内设置分段。
Paragraph 2
The method is often applied to large orebodies with steep dips. The ore is strong enough to support the stope walls采场侧墙 and the country rock is also strong.
该方法通常用于急倾斜的大型矿体。矿石强度足够支撑采场侧墙，围岩强度也足够。
Method 方法
Paragraph 3
The stope is divided into sublevels分段 at regular intervals. Longhole drilling深孔凿岩 is used to blast the ore in slices from the top downward.
采场按一定间距划分为分段。采用深孔凿岩从上向下分层爆破矿石。
Paragraph 4
The broken ore falls to the bottom of the stope and is drawn off through drawpoints出矿口 located at the bottom of the stope.
破碎矿石落到采场底部，通过采场底部的出矿口放出。
Advantages 优点
Paragraph 5
Sublevel stopping is a highly productive高效率 method. It provides good fragmentation破碎效果 and low dilution贫化.
分段空场法是一种高效率采矿方法。它提供良好的破碎效果和低贫化率。
Paragraph 6
The method is suitable for orebodies with regular boundaries and strong ore and rock. It requires good planning规划 and sequencing顺序安排 of mining operations.
该方法适用于边界规则、矿石和岩石强度大的矿体。它需要良好的规划和采矿作业顺序安排。
