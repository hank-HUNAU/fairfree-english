Stope and Pillar vs Room and Pillar
全面法与房柱法比较
Dr.Z
What are the main differences between stope and pillar全面法 mining and room and pillar房柱法 mining?
全面法和房柱法的主要区别是什么？
The pillars in stope and pillar全面法 are irregularly shaped形状不规则 and randomly located. The deposit is moderately thick to thick, over 6 meters.
全面法的矿柱形状不规则，位置不固定。矿体为中厚至厚，超过6米。

2
Pillar Placement Strategy
矿柱布置策略
Dr.Z
What is the objective in placement of pillars in stope and pillar mining?
全面法中矿柱布置的目标是什么？
The objective is to locate pillars in low-grade ore贫矿 or waste废石, so long as adequate roof support顶板支护 is provided.
目标是将矿柱布置在贫矿或废石中，只要能提供足够的顶板支护。
Mr.L
Dr.Z
Why are fewer pillars required in stope and pillar mining?
为什么全面法需要的矿柱较少？
Because the pillars consist of rock, they are relatively strong. Rock pillars can support more load.
因为矿柱由岩石组成，强度相对较高。岩石矿柱能承受更大荷载。
Mr.L
Key Vocabulary
low-grade ore 贫矿
waste 废石
roof support 顶板支护
rock pillars 岩石矿柱
3
Development Requirements
采准要求讨论
Dr.Z
Why can development采准 be sharply reduced in stope and pillar mining?
为什么全面法的采准工作大幅减少？
First, strict laws for multiple openings and ventilation in coal mining don't apply to hard-rock mining硬岩开采. Second, deposits are more irregularly shaped.
首先，煤矿的多作业面和通风严格法律不适用于硬岩开采。其次，矿床形状不规整。
Mr.L
Dr.Z
What is the typical mining direction in stope and pillar?
全面法的典型采矿方向是什么？
Mining on the advance is the rule. Development openings cannot always be driven in ore.
前进式开采是常规做法。采准巷道不一定能在矿体内掘进。
Mr.L
Key Vocabulary
development 采准
hard-rock mining 硬岩开采
mining on the advance 前进式开采
irregular deposit 不规整矿床
