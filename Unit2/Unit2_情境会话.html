Scene 01
竖井与开拓 — Shaft and Development
两位工程师讨论大型深部矿体的基本结构、竖井开拓方式、矿石损失与提升设备。
Z Dr. Zhang — 地质工程师
L Mr. Li — 采矿工程师
Z
Dr. Zhang
Mr. Li, let's discuss the basic structure of a mine. Large ore-bodies are often known as massive巨大的, but the term massive should not be confused with massive and disseminated ores块状矿与浸染矿 in a geological sense.
L
Mr. Li
I see. So a massive ore-body is likely to contain disseminated ores浸染矿, while a vein-type ore-body脉型矿体 will probably contain bands of massive ore minerals.
Z
Dr. Zhang
Exactly. Now, a shaft竖井 is sunk开凿 alongside or through the ore-body for access. In practice, there must be two openings to allow air to be forced through the mine workings.
L
Mr. Li
What happens if the shaft is sunk through the ore-body?
Z
Dr. Zhang
If the shaft is sunk through the ore-body, then some of the ore is sterilized报废. A cone of ore surrounding the shaft and widening with depth must be left until the rest of the ore is extracted.
L
Mr. Li
That makes sense. Otherwise the movement of rock caused by ore extraction will damage the shaft and necessitate expensive repairs.
Z
Dr. Zhang
There are two types of conveyance提升容器 in the shafts: cages罐笼 and skips箕斗. Cages are used for man-riding and to wind mine cars containing ore, waste rock or materials.
L
Mr. Li
So a large shaft may be equipped with a pair of skips for ore and rock hoisting and a single cage for man riding.

张博士：李先生，我们来讨论一下矿山的基本结构。大型矿体通常被称为巨大的，但"巨大"一词不应与地质学意义上的块状矿和浸染矿混淆。
李先生：我明白了。所以巨大的矿体可能含有浸染矿，而脉型矿体则可能含有块状矿石的条带。
张博士：没错。竖井是沿着矿体或在矿体中开凿以供出入的。实际上，必须有两个开口，以便将空气压入矿井巷道中。
李先生：如果竖井穿过矿体开凿会怎样？
张博士：如果竖井穿过矿体开凿，那么部分矿石就会被报废。围绕竖井并向深处扩展的锥形矿石必须保留，直到其余矿石被采出。
李先生：有道理。否则矿石开采引起的岩体移动会损坏竖井，需要进行昂贵的维修。
张博士：竖井中有两种提升容器：罐笼和箕斗。罐笼用于载人以及提升装有矿石、废石或材料的矿车。
李先生：所以大型竖井可能配备一对箕斗用于矿石和岩石提升，以及一个罐笼用于载人。

Scene 02
矿体形态与开拓 — Orebody Types and Development
两位工程师讨论脉型矿体的走向、倾角、上下盘稳定性及斜井与竖井的开拓选择。
Z Dr. Zhang — 地质工程师
L Mr. Li — 采矿工程师
Z
Dr. Zhang
A large ore-body may in fact consist of clusters群集 of smaller ore-bodies of a vein矿脉-type, and development may be on a regular grid.
L
Mr. Li
Alternatively或者, the ore-body may be one predominant主要的 vein which is long and narrow on its strike走向 direction and which extends to the dip for a considerable depth.
Z
Dr. Zhang
Technically, the strike is at right angles to the full dip. But within an orebody the strike may follow a sinuous蜿蜒的 line and the dip frequently varies.
L
Mr. Li
I understand. So we have to take general trends into account. But what about the hanging wall上盘 stability?
Z
Dr. Zhang
Mining operations would weaken the hanging wall, that is, the rock lying above the ore body, and cause it to collapse. As a result, the major developments are normally made in the footwall下盘.
L
Mr. Li
So the footwall is below the orebody where the rock is more stable. That's why main developments are placed there.
Z
Dr. Zhang
Correct. In the early stages, an inclined shaft斜井 may be driven down from the surface and progressively逐步地 deepened to follow ore extraction.
L
Mr. Li
But haulage speeds are generally lower and maintenance costs are higher in inclined shafts. So a change will be made to vertical shaft winding at greater depths.

张博士：大型矿体实际上可能由脉型的较小矿体群集组成，开拓可以按规则网格进行。
李先生：或者，矿体可能是一条主要矿脉，在其走向方向上又长又窄，并向倾斜方向延伸到相当深的地方。
张博士：从技术上讲，走向与全倾角成直角。但在矿体内，走向可能沿蜿蜒的线条延伸，倾角也经常变化。
李先生：我理解了。所以我们必须考虑总体趋势。但上盘的稳定性呢？
张博士：采矿作业会削弱上盘，即位于矿体上方的岩石，并导致其坍塌。因此，主要开拓工程通常布置在下盘。
李先生：所以下盘在矿体下方，岩石更稳定。这就是为什么主要开拓工程布置在那里。
张博士：正确。在早期阶段，可以从地表向下掘进斜井，并逐步加深以跟随矿石开采。
李先生：但斜井中的运输速度通常较低，维护成本较高。因此在更大深度会改为竖井提升。

Scene 03
浅部矿体与露天开采 — Shallow Orebodies and Open Pit
两位工程师讨论浅部矿体的露天开采方式、台阶设计、运输系统及平巷开拓。
Z Dr. Zhang — 地质工程师
L Mr. Li — 采矿工程师
Z
Dr. Zhang
Shallow orebodies are worked as open pit mining operations wherever possible. The scale of equipment is generally smaller in metalliferous金属的 orebodies than in shallow bedded deposits.
L
Mr. Li
That's because of the need for bench台阶ing downwards. A center cut or drop cut is made in the pit bottom and a bench is taken outward.
Z
Dr. Zhang
The overall inclination of the pit walls is generally about 45 degrees and bench heights are generally about 15 meters. As the pit becomes deeper, more waste rock or overburden覆盖层 has to be removed.
L
Mr. Li
Eventually economics force a change to underground mining methods. What about haulage out of the pit?
Z
Dr. Zhang
Haulage is at first by truck in a spiral around the benches. It may then become more economic to drop the ore through a crusher to an underground haulage.
L
Mr. Li
The access tunnel driven down through the strata to the bottom of the open pit is called a drift平巷, isn't it?
Z
Dr. Zhang
Yes. A drift is used for rail or conveyor haulage at grades of up to 1 in 2.5. With locomotive haulage the grades are restricted to less than 1 in 15.
L
Mr. Li
For large scale mechanized mining, hauling ore by low-profile diesel truck低矮柴油卡车s up an access spiral is probably cheaper than hauling to a shaft and hoisting.

张博士：浅部矿体尽可能采用露天开采方式。金属矿体的设备规模通常比浅部层状矿床小。
李先生：这是因为需要向下分层台阶式开采。在坑底进行中心切割或下切，然后向外推进台阶。
张博士：坑壁的整体倾角一般为45度左右，台阶高度一般约为15米。随着采坑加深，需要剥离更多的废石或覆盖层。
李先生：最终经济因素会迫使转向地下开采方法。那采坑的运输呢？
张博士：最初是卡车沿台阶螺旋运输。之后将矿石通过破碎机落入地下运输系统可能更经济。
李先生：穿过岩层向下掘进到露天采坑底部的通道巷道叫做平巷，对吗？
张博士：是的。平巷用于铁路或输送机运输，坡度可达1:2.5。机车运输的坡度限制在小于1:15。
李先生：对于大规模机械化开采，用低矮柴油卡车沿出入螺旋运输矿石可能比运到竖井再提升更便宜。
