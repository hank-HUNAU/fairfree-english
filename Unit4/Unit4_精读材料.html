Paragraph 3
The water can be dealt with by chemical injectionchemical injection, groutinggrouting with cement, or freezingfreezing. These methods have all been applied at inclinations from vertical to horizontal. The cost is obviously directly proportional to the lineal meter treated.
通过化学注浆，水泥灌浆或冷冻法可以解决水的问题。根据实际情况，这些堵水的方法在竖井掘进和斜井掘进中都有应用。很显然，堵水成本与堵水井筒的长度成正比。
Paragraph 4
In practice 'weak' ground is where the nature of the rock, particularly its broken condition and where deterioration of clayey materials occurs, causes frequent falls of roof and sides. The intensive support needed makes drivagedrivage of inclines particularly expensive.
在实际工程中，低强度岩石是指特别容易破碎，且含有黏土物质的容易破碎的岩石。这种岩石通常引起采场顶板和侧墙的冒落。这种岩石需要大量支护，而且影响生产能力，在这种岩石中掘进斜井的成本特别高。
Paragraph 5
Loose surficialsurficial deposits may comprise running sands, loose gravel, soft clay, or peat bogspeat bogs. In these deposits a vertical shaft which gives a balanced hydrostatichydrostatic loading on the lininglining is safest and strongest.
松散表层沉积物可能包括流砂、松散砾石、软黏土或泥炭沼泽。在这些沉积物中，竖井可以给衬砌提供平衡的静水压力载荷，是最安全、最坚固的。
